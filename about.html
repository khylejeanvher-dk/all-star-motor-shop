    WHO WE ARE

    ALL STAR
    MOTOR SHOP

    Pasig City's most trusted destination for motorcycles, parts, and the culture that drives them. Since 2010, we've been fueling the ride.

    OUR STORY

    BORN FROM A PASSION FOR THE RIDE

    ALL STAR MOTOR SHOP started as a dream in a small garage in Pasig City. Founded by motorcycle enthusiasts who were tired of compromising on quality and service, we set out to build something different — a shop that treats every rider like family and every bike like a work of art.

    Over a decade later, we've grown into one of Metro Manila's premier motorcycle dealerships, carrying a handpicked selection of sport bikes, adventure tourers, naked bikes, cruisers, and everything in between. Our parts and accessories catalog is the most comprehensive in the region.

    Whether you're picking up your first bike or adding to a growing collection, we're here to help you find the perfect ride — and keep it running like new.

    WHAT DRIVES US

    QUALITY FIRST

    Every bike and part we carry is vetted for quality and reliability. We only stock products we would put on our own bikes.

    RIDERS FIRST

    Our staff are riders themselves. We give honest advice, not pushy sales pitches — because we know what it means to find the right bike.

    COMMUNITY

    We're more than a shop — we're a community hub for Pasig riders. Group rides, maintenance workshops, and events happen here.
